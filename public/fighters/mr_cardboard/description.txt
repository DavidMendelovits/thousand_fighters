Mr Cardboard is a high-quality pixel-art arcade fighter with an orange-red cap, neat moustache, black-and-white checker shirt, huge dark green overalls, striped socks, and tan boots. The overalls are widened by a hidden internal support under the fabric, creating a sharp horizontal lower-belly shelf and crisp angular side corners without any visible external rod. His attacks use handmade corrugated cardboard props: sword, sweep board, giant mallet, and Cardbross Cross.
