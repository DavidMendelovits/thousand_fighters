The Rubber Chicken is a classic yellow rubber chicken toy come to life, with a bumpy yellow body, long neck, red comb and wattle, orange beak, orange feet, silly blue eyes, and a green collar. It fights through squeaks, floppy wings, stretched rubber limbs, and absurd elastic recoil.

Silhouette: tall skinny rubber chicken with long neck, tiny wings, and orange feet.
Clothing: none, toy body only.
Hair: red rubber comb.
Accessories: green collar band.
Attitude: frantic, squeaky, ridiculous, surprisingly dangerous.
Distinctive traits: elastic neck snaps, rubber wobble, squeak-ring projectile effects.
