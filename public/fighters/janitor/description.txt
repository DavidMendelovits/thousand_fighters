Silhouette: solid, grounded, hunched forward with a long mop extension.
Clothing: blue-gray janitor coveralls and black shoes.
Accessories: long mop, bucket, wet-floor gag props.
Attitude: stern, practical, unexpectedly dangerous.
Distinctive traits: bald head, gray beard, mop-as-bo-staff weapon.
